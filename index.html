About

Denim 909 is the live lo-fi house project of Mimbot — performing original tracks in real time with Ableton Live and hardware, paired with live visuals designed for intimate club spaces.

Denim 909 is a live lo-fi house project by Mimbot, an electronic musician with a background in hardware-driven live performance. After years of performing ambient and IDM-influenced sets with drum machines and synthesizers, the project evolved toward groove-focused, dancefloor-ready music while maintaining a hands-on live approach. Denim 909 is built entirely from original material and performed live with Ableton Live, combining warm, dusty house textures with generative visuals for immersive late-night performances.
